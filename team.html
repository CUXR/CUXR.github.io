Our Team

CUXR is dedicated to forming a dynamic team centered around diverse perspectives and talents in the fields of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Extended Reality (XR). By bringing together individuals with a wide range of skills and backgrounds, we foster a collaborative environment where creativity and innovation can thrive. Together, we aim to push the boundaries of what's possible, building projects that shape the future of immersive technology.
