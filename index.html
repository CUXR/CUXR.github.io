Who We Are


Founded in Spring 2024, we are a team of undergraduate students at Cornell University exploring and creating software, hardware, games, and events for Virtual, Augmented, Mixed Reality, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)! We aim to form a team centered around various perspectives and talents around HCI and XR and we aim to strive towards building innovative projects.

Projects in XR and HCI

Our members work on hands-on projects spanning VR game development, creating haptic interfaces, and developing HCI research projects.

From working VR game development, CAD modeling, PCB design, and custom hardware fabrication, our teams tackle the full stack of XR development.

Every project is student-led, student-driven, and built with the goal of pushing what's possible at the intersection of humans and XR.

Industry Collaborations

CUXR actively bridges the gap between student innovation and industry practice.

We've hosted guest talks and information sessions with professionals from companies involved in XR and HCI, and have pursued sponsored project opportunities that connect our work to real-world problems.

These relationships shape how we build, what we prioritize, and how we grow as an organization.
