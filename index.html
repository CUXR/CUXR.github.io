About the Team

Who We Are

Founded in Spring 2024, we are a team of undergraduate students at Cornell University exploring and creating software, hardware, games, and events for Virtual, Augmented, Mixed Reality, and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)!

What We Do

  • Lead student-driven projects in XR and HCI
  • Develop innovative software, hardware, and games
  • Collaborate with faculty and industry experts
  • Host events and workshops

This organization is a registered student organization of Cornell University.
